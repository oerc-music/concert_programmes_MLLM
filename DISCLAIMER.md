# Disclaimer

## Experimental software

This software is an **experimental demonstration tool** prepared for the 13th International Conference on Digital Libraries for Musicology (DLfM 2026). It is not finalised, peer-reviewed, or publishable research software. It is provided for demonstration and research exploration purposes only.

Annotations produced by this tool are **unverified, AI-generated first-pass drafts** and must not be treated as authoritative catalogue metadata.

## Data and images

**Images leave your machine** when you run a live annotation. Each programme image you submit is transmitted to Google (or whichever provider you configure) per that provider's terms of service. Review your provider's data use and retention policy before processing third-party or sensitive material.
